16_AUGUST_2025: “The Big Green Thing” is a name which karbytes assigned to a continuous region of land near Oakland, California comprised of the following smaller territories: (0) Las Trampas Wilderness (which is south of Walnut Creek, west of Danville, north of San Ramon, and east of Castro Valley), (1) Ramage Peak Trail & Dinosaur Ridge Trail (which is directly accesible from Chabot Staging Area along Redwood Road north of Lake Chabot Regional Park in Castro Valley (and which borders the Upper San Leandro Reservoir)), (2) Lake Chabot Regional Park (which is a region encomassing Anthony Chabot Campgrounds, Lake Chabot, and Anthony Chabot Dam and which is south of Chabot Staging Area and borders Redwood Canyon Golf Course), and (3) “Horsey Hill” (which is a small hilly patch of wilderness where cows sometimes graze bordered by Lake Chabot Road and Crest Avenue and which is immediately south of Lake Chabot Regional Park).
